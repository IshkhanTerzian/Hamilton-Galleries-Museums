This website incorporates an interactive Google Map, where special markers are placed
to indicate Hamilton's Museums and Galleries across the region.

Buttons are layed out to find the users geolocation on the map as well as a drop down menu
to find the direction distance from the users current location to the end distance.

A direction panel is incorported once the user decides where the they want to travel to.
Direction panel uses the biclying feature.
